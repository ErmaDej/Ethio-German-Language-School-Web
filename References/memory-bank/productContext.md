# Product Context: EthioGerman Language School

## The Problem
Ethiopian students and professionals seeking to study or work in German-speaking countries face a significant hurdle: mastering the German language to CEFR standards (A1-C2). Existing solutions often lack specialized local context (Addis Ababa), flexible delivery modes, and integrated cultural education.

## The Solution
A specialized German language teaching platform that:
- Focused exclusively on German language education (CEFR A1-C2).
- Offers three delivery modes: Online, Onsite (Addis Ababa), and One-to-One.
- Integrates German cultural education with language learning.
- Provides a modern, interactive student and instructor experience.

## Target Audience
- Ethiopian students planning to study in Germany/Austria/Switzerland.
- Professionals seeking work opportunities in German-speaking regions.
- Language enthusiasts in Addis Ababa.

## Key Goals
- Establish market leadership in specialized German education in Ethiopia.
- Provide a seamless digital experience for enrollment, learning, and tracking progress.
- Bridge the cultural gap between Ethiopia and Germany through technology.
